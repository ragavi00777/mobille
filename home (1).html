ELECTERA-25

Electera is the annual national level technical symposium of the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Coimbatore Institute of Technology (CIT). Established in 2004, Electera has grown to become one of the most prestigious technical events in South India, attracting participation from top engineering colleges across the country. The symposium serves as a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and innovation through a variety of events, workshops, and competitions. With a focus on fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing, Electera provides an opportunity for students to interact with industry experts, academicians, and fellow enthusiasts. The event not only promotes technical excellence but also encourages holistic development by incorporating cultural and social activities. Over the years, Electera has earned a reputation for its well-organized events, cutting-edge themes, and enthusiastic participation, making it a highlight in the academic calendar of CIT and a significant contributor to the technical community.
